You upload your resume and pick a role
The system reads your resume and extracts your skills and tech stack
It searches a role-specific knowledge base and pulls the most relevant content
That content + your profile gets sent to GPT to generate questions made for you
You answer each question in the UI and get instant AI feedback
At the end you get a full summary — score, strengths, weak areas, and a hiring recommendation
Everything is saved to the database throughout the session

That's it. Resume in → personalized interview → scored results out
